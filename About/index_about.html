David is a Dublin-born director who has been shooting internationally for a variety of renowned brands and clients. He has received a variety of award recognition including the Cannes Young Director award and the Best New Director in Ireland award at the Kinsale Sharks. He is currently represented at Aylene Gardiner Agency. After shooting an abundance of travel-documentary mostly within freestyle snowsports, his thirst for adventure is combined with a passion for narrative drama. Imaginative storytelling and visual flair bring his films to life and whatever the situation or the budget, his focus is primarily on great work.


David is represented by Aylene Gardiner Agency.

  • Cannes Young Director Award (nom)
  • Best New Director in Ireland at The Kinsale Sharks (nom)
  • Talenthouse Way of Play award (win)
  • Best film in Competition 3rd place, mofilm (win)
