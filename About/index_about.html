David is a Dublin-born & London-based director who has been shooting internationally for a variety of renowned brands and clients. With a previous life as a freestyle snow-sports filmmaker for Team GB (with over 5 million views) his thirst for adventure is combined with a passion for narrative drama. Imaginative storytelling and visual flair help bring his films to life. Whatever the budget, his focus is primarily on great work!


David is represented by Aylene Gardiner Agency.

Awards include:

  • Cannes Young Director Award (nom)
  • Best New Director in Ireland at The Kinsale Sharks (nom)
  • Talenthouse Way of Play award (win)
  • Best film in Competition 3rd place, mofilm (win)
